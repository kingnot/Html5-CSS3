Beyond The Song

- The lyrics describe placing a call to someone in the middle of the night due to being lonely and longing for companionship.

- Hillary Scott commented on the song, saying that All three of us know what it's like to get to that point where you feel lonely enough that you make a late night phone call that you very well could regret the next day.

- Charles Kelley told The Boot that the band's record executives initially had concerns regarding using the lyrics "I'm a little drunk", but convinced the executives to leave the content in the song.
